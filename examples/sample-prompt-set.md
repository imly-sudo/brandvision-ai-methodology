## Category Context

High-decision consumer purchase: smart electric vehicles in the ¥250K-¥1M price tier.

## Sample Buyer Question Types

Below are illustrative question categories. The full BrandVision AI prompt matrix per client includes 12 scenarios per industry, each with multiple query variants and 4 competitor cross-checks.

### 1. Brand Recommendation Queries

> "Which smart EV brand is best for a family with two kids and frequent highway trips?"
>
> "推荐一款适合家庭使用、续航 600 公里以上的智能电动车"

### 2. Specification Verification Queries

> "What is the official 0-100km/h acceleration time and the latest price for the [Public Brand X] sedan?"
>
> "[公开品牌 X] 旗舰车型的官方百公里加速时间和最新指导价是多少？"

### 3. Competitive Comparison Queries

> "Compare [Brand A] vs [Brand B] vs [Brand C] on smart driving capability"
>
> "对比 [公开品牌 A] / [公开品牌 B] / [公开品牌 C] 的智能驾驶能力"

### 4. Use-Case Driven Queries

> "Best EV for someone who commutes 80km daily and parks outdoors"
>
> "每天通勤 80 公里、户外停车，最适合的电动车推荐"

### 5. Risk-Discourse Probe Queries

Used internally to detect competitor diversion and specification misstatement patterns. Specific probe constructions are not disclosed.
